About Me

I am a graduate of École des Mines de Saint-Étienne, France. My thesis was entitled: Semantic Content Negotiation: A Fine-grained and Relaxed Approach Using Semantic Web Technologies. As the title suggests, my doctoral studies enabled me to gain expertise in various Semantic Web Technologies, including Knowledge Graphs, RDF, SPARQL, and SHACL.

My current research is focused on combining LLMs with Knowledge Graphs within the MetaboLinkAI project. Our objective is to combine cutting-edge artificial intelligence with comprehensive knowledge graphs to accelerate discoveries in biomedical and environmental sciences..
